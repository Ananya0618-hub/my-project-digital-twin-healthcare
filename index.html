A smart healthcare system that creates a digital twin of patients by storing medical history, hospital visits and treatment records for real-time monitoring and better diagnosis.

Project Overview

Digital Twin Healthcare is a database-driven system that maintains patient records, hospital visits and treatment history. It helps hospitals digitally track patients and provides quick access to medical data for better decision making.

Key Features

Patient Records

Store Aadhaar, name, DOB and contact details

Hospital Visits

Track patient visit history and reasons

Treatment Records

Store diagnosis and treatment details

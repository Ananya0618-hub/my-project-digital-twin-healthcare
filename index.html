Problem Statement

Modern healthcare systems store patient data in fragmented systems across hospitals, labs and clinics. Patients and doctors do not have access to a unified lifetime health record. This results in repeated tests, inefficient diagnosis, delayed treatments and lack of predictive healthcare insights.

There is a need for a centralized digital twin healthcare system that continuously mirrors patient health, provides real-time monitoring and helps doctors make data-driven decisions.

Project Description

HealthMirror is a Digital Twin Healthcare platform that creates a virtual representation of a patient’s health profile by integrating medical history, lab reports, treatments and risk predictions.

The system allows patients to store and view lifetime medical data and enables doctors to monitor patient health, prescribe treatments and analyze trends. It provides a unified platform for predictive healthcare using digital twin technology.

Literature Review

  • Digital Twin technology has been widely used in Industry 4.0 and is now emerging in healthcare.
  • Research shows digital twins improve predictive diagnosis and personalized treatment planning.
  • Existing hospital systems lack unified patient lifetime data tracking.
  • Healthcare dashboards help doctors analyze patient health trends and risks.
  • Secure centralized medical data platforms improve healthcare efficiency.

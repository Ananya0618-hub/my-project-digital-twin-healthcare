HealthMirror – Digital Twin Healthcare

A centralized digital twin platform to monitor lifetime health records, predict risks and assist doctors with real-time patient insights.

By Ananya Gupta

Reg No: 2427030166

Guide: Dr Sayar Singh Shekhawat

Problem Statement

Healthcare data today is fragmented across hospitals, clinics and labs. Patients lack a unified health record, while doctors cannot access complete medical history instantly. This leads to repeated tests, delayed diagnosis and inefficient treatment planning.

A Digital Twin Healthcare System is needed to create a virtual representation of patient health that continuously updates and assists in predictive healthcare.

Project Description

HealthMirror is a digital twin healthcare platform that maintains a virtual replica of patient health data including medical history, lab reports, medications and risk analysis. The system provides dashboards for both patients and doctors to monitor and manage healthcare efficiently.

Literature Review

Digital Twin technology enables real-time monitoring of systems.
Healthcare analytics improves diagnosis and treatment.
Centralized EHR systems improve efficiency.
Predictive healthcare reduces long-term risks.

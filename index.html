Digital Twin Healthcare System

A centralized healthcare platform that creates a lifelong digital twin of a patient by integrating hospital visits, treatments, lab reports, and AI-driven insights.

Problem Statement

Healthcare data is fragmented across hospitals and clinics, leading to inefficient diagnosis, repeated tests, and delayed treatment decisions. Doctors lack access to a unified patient history, while patients have limited control over their medical records.

Project Description

This project proposes a Digital Twin Healthcare System that creates a virtual representation of a patient’s medical lifecycle. It stores hospital visits, treatments, lab reports, and prescriptions in a centralized database accessible by both patients and doctors.

Literature Review

  • Electronic Health Record (EHR) systems
  • Digital Twin concepts in healthcare
  • AI-assisted clinical decision support systems
  • Centralized healthcare data platforms

Objectives

  • Build a centralized healthcare database
  • Create a lifelong digital twin for patients
  • Enable doctor–patient data access
  • Provide scalable backend architecture

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Backend: Node.js, Express
  • Database: MySQL
